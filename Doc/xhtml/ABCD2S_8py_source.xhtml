16  """Converts ABCD parameters to s-parameters.\n
17  @param ABCD list of list representing ABCD matrix to convert.
18  @param Z0 (optional) float or complex or list of reference impedance (defaults to None).
19  @param K (optional) float or complex or list of scaling factor (defaults to None).
20  @note
21  Supports only two-port devices.\n
22  @see Z0KHelper to see how the reference impedance
23  and scaling factor are determined.
24  """
25  (Z0,K)=Z0KHelper((Z0,K),len(ABCD))
26  Z01=Z0.item(0,0)
27  Z02=Z0.item(1,1)
28  K1=K.item(0,0)
29  K2=K.item(1,1)
30  C11=matrix([[0,0],[1.0/(2.0*K2),Z02/(2.0*K2)]])
31  C12=matrix([[1.0/(2.0*K1),-Z01/(2.0*K1)],[0,0]])
32  C21=matrix([[0,0],[1.0/(2.0*K2),-Z02/(2.0*K2)]])
33  C22=matrix([[1.0/(2.0*K1),Z01/(2.0*K1)],[0,0]])
34  return array((C21+C22*ABCD)*((C11+C12*ABCD).getI())).tolist()
