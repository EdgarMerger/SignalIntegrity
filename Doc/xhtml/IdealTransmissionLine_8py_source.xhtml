12  """IdealTransmissionLine
13  Ideal Transmission Line
14  @param rho float or complex reflection coefficient
15  @param gamma float or complex propagation constant
16  @return the s-parameter matrix of an ideal two port transmission line
17  rho is defined for a characteristic impedance Zc as (Zc-Z0)/(Zc+Z0)
18  """
19  L = cmath.exp(-gamma)
20  S11=rho*(1.-L*L)/(1.-rho*rho*L*L)
21  S21=(1.-rho*rho)*L/(1.-rho*rho*L*L)
22  return [[S11,S21],[S21,S11]]
